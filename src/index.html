About the App

Stormwater runoff from construction sites can cause significant harm to our rivers, lakes, and coastal waters. A SWPPP inspection is required (by your construction general permit) and will help you prevent stormwater pollution. A SWPPP is more than just a sediment and erosion control plan. It describes all the construction site operator’s activities to prevent stormwater contamination, control sedimentation and erosion, and comply with the requirements of the Clean Water Act. This app is fully customizable to fit your documenting needs, and works offline in environments with no / low internet signal.
